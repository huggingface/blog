# Reachy Mini – The Open-Source Robot for Today's and Tomorrow's AI Builders


A tiny, affordable robot built by [**Hugging Face**](https://huggingface.co/) and [**Pollen Robotics**](https://www.pollen-robotics.com/) to make AI-powered robotics accessible to everyone:  developers, educators, researchers, and tinkerers.

Reachy Mini is an expressive, open-source robot designed for human-robot interaction, creative coding, and AI experimentation. It’s fully programmable in Python (and soon JavaScript, Scratch) and priced from **$299.** Whether you're an AI developer, hacker, researcher, teacher, robot enthusiast, or just coding with your kids on the weekend, Reachy Mini lets you develop, test, deploy, and share real-world AI applications from your desk, using the latest AI models!

[👉 Discover apps, models & community projects on the Reachy Mini Hub](https://huggingface.co/reachy-mini)

### **🧠 Built for Exploring, Playing, Learning & Sharing**

---

### **🤝 Designed for Human-Robot Interaction**

Reachy Mini is designed for **human-robot interaction**:

- Its **6DOF head** and **animated antennas** let it express curiosity, surprise, or focus.
- The **wide-angle camera** and **4 microphones** enable face tracking, sound localization, and voice chat.
- The **5W speaker** gives it a voice - warm, clear, and full of character.

And yes: it can dance. (But we’ll get to that.)

### **🌍 Open Source, Open Future**

We’re committed to openness - in code, in design, in community.

- 🤗 **Hugging Face integration**: Use any open [apps](https://huggingface.co/reachy-mini#/apps) and discover new ways to interact with your robot.
- 📦 **Open-source SDK**: [Available on GitHub](https://github.com/pollen-robotics/reachy_mini), with Python support and more languages coming.
- 🧪 **Simulation**: Develop and test in MuJoCo using models shared by the community - [all available here](https://huggingface.co/reachy-mini#/apps).
- 🔧 **Hardware**: Full CAD files and schematics will be released soon.

This isn’t just a product - it’s a **platform for collective innovation**.
